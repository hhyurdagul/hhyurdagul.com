# SEO Checklist for Static Sites

A short checklist you can use for future posts.

## Page-level

- One clear `#` title per page
- First paragraph summarizes the page (used as meta description)
- Internal links are consistent with clean URLs

## Technical

- Canonical URLs are correct
- `robots.txt` and `sitemap.xml` exist
